ABOUT

This is a Blackjack game built in C++,
by Jean, Ethan, and Jack.

Web interface powered by vanilla JavaScript.

GAME RULES

Player 1 and Player 2 take turns playing Blackjack.

The player closer to 21 without busting wins the round.

Each player has a face-down card revealed only when they Stand.

First player to 3 points wins the game.

If both bust or tie, the round is a Draw.
